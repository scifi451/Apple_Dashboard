iPhone



You can say that there has been no other product from any other technology company that has been as successful as the iPhone has been over the last decade or more given the heights it has reached in its revenue at 61.58 billion in fiscal quarter one of 2018. While it might not have the rocket start that the iPad had at its debut it has consistently come to define Apple as a company since it's taken over the biggest product that Apple now makes in terms of revenue and number of devices sold. Another way to show how dominant the iPhone has been is that from fiscal quarter two 2008 to fiscal quarter one 2016 the iPhone category had 31 straight quarters of year over year growth in revenue.

To get a true grasp on how big just the iPhone category is by itself look at the Fortune 500 rank for 2019. The iPhone category itself would rank as the 14th largest Fortune 500 company which is just staggering to think about, considering how large it is within that context compared to some other whole companies.
