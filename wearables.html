Wearables



Realistically the wearables category is driven by two main products Apple Watch and Airpods. The Apple Watch has had a real growth spurt in the last year and even further helped by the fact that in September 2019 Apple dropped the older model Apple Watch Series 3 down to a new starting price of $199 helping to make it more attractive to consumers. Also driving growth in this category is the Airpods in that deputed in December 2016 and then the second model noise-canceling version Airpods Pro in October 2019. Fiscal quarter one 2020 had a 37% year over year growth and marks the tenth straight quarter that this category has had over 30% year over year growth.
